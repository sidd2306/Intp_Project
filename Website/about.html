Welcome to our website- Cooking for youngsters

This is a universally acknowledged fact that food is one of the basic needs of everyone.

Firstly, many parents do not allow their kids to leave their dwelling for higher studies as they are normally worried about their food habits. Secondly, if children know how to cook the food, maybe in case of emergency when their parents have to leave the house they are less dependent on the outside food. Finally, for those students who are currently staying in the hostel, cooking skill will be an advantage, otherwise for eating they need to rely on outside or fast foods. Moreover, eating junk foods can affect their health.

Thus our website cooking for youngsters is an aid to students or youngsters who want to know how to cook. We have a lot of basic recipes in our recipe magazine which will help youngsters to cherish their food cravings in quick time, with a very few ingredients and in a healthy way by not relying on outside food. We also have a basic cooking videos option wherein very basic recipes like rice, chapati, dal,etc have been explained more comprehensively with the help of videos and video links.

There is also a cooking tips option where a list of tips which every cook should follow when cooking or learning how to cook has been listed in a categorized order

We also have a tiffin delivery service started by our website where we provide veg and non-veg lunch on a monthly basis for youngsters who cannot take out time for making their meal. For more details refer the tiffin delivery tab

Everyone an reach out to us with their feedbacks,suggestions,requests and complaints in the contact us section. We hope to hear you soon and give a prompt reply
