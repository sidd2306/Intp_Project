Kheer


Ingredients:

  1. 500 ml Milk (Doodh)
  2. 250 gms Rice (Chawal)
  3. 200 gms Sugar (Cheeni)
  4. 50 gms Ghee
  5. 3 tblsp chopped Nuts

Procedure:

  1. Pick and wash the rice.
  2. Soak in water for 30 minutes.
  3. Drain completely.
  4. Melt the ghee in a pan, add the drained rice and saute for 5-7 minutes on a medium flame.
  5. add the milk and cook on a low flame till the milk is reduced to 3/4th its original quantity.
  6. Add the sugar and simmer further till the milk is reduced to half its original quantity.
  7. Garnish with nuts.

Fruit Salad


Ingredients:

  1. Grapes - 200 grams
  2. Pomegranate - 1
  3. Mango - 1
  4. Apple - 1
  5. Cream - 1 cup (200 grams)
  6. Sugar - 3/4 cup (150 grams)
  7. Vanilla custard - more than 1/4 cup
  8. Milk - 1 ltr (full cream)

Procedure:

  1. Take milk in any vessel and place it on flame. Keep 3/4 cup cold milk for later use.
  2. Add custard powder in cold milk and stir until all lumps dissolve. After 4-5 minutes, when milk starts simmering, add custard batter steadily.
  3. Keep stirring the milk with help of a spoon. Add entire custard batter and mix nicely. Add sugar as well.
  4. Stir the milk-custard batter rigorously and cook for 7-8 minutes until it gets thick in consistency.
  5. Churn the cream and cut the fruits
  6. Add cream and fruits to the custard and serve chilled
