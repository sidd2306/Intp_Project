Omlette


Ingredients:

  1. 2 Eggs
  2. 1 meduim sized chopped Onion (Pyaj)
  3. 1 small sized chopped Tomato (Tamatar)
  4. 2 chopped Green Chilli (Hari mirch)
  5. Salt to taste
  6. 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  7. A pinch of Curry Powder
  8. 1/2 tsp Cumin Seed Powder (Jeera)
  9. 1 tsp Olive Oil

Procedure:

  1. Beat the eggs thoroughly in a basin.
  2. Add the onions, tomatoes and the green chillies. mix thoroughly.
  3. Add the garam masala powder, curry powder and salt. mix well.
  4. Heat the oil in a non stick pan and add the cumin seeds.fry for a few seconds over a medium flame.
  5. Pour the egg mixture and cook on both sides till light brown in colour.( reduce the flame) serve hot.

Tomato soup


Ingredients:

  1. 1 1/2 lbs. of ripe tomatoes remove seeds, peeled and chopped
  2. 2 tbsp of Olive Oil
  3. 1 Onion chopped
  4. 1 Clove of garlic crushed
  5. 1 Small red pepper chopped (optional)
  6. 4 Cups of chicken or vegetable stock
  7. 2 oz of tomato paste
  8. 1/4 cup of Basil

Procedure:

  1. Heat the oil in a heavy pan, cook the onion, garlic and red pepper till soft.
  2. Add the tomato and cook another 10 minutes.
  3. Add the tomato paste, salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cover and let simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Allow to cool slightly and put the mixture in a blender until it is smooth.
  6. Return to the pan and heat gradually.
  7. Add the fresh basil to the tomato soup, cook 2 minutes and serve.

Poha


Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups Poha (Beaten Rice)
  2. 1 Potatoes
  3. 1 Onions
  4. 2 Green Chillies
  5. 1 tsp Chana dal
  6. 1 tsp Urad dal
  7. 1/4 tsp Mustard Seeds
  8. 1 sprig Curry leaves
  9. 2 tsp Peanuts
  10. 4 tblsp Oil
  11. 1 pinch Turmeric powder
  12. 1 Lemon
  13. Few Corainder leaves
  14. Salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. Soak the poha in water. Wash and drain all the water.
  2. Add some salt , turmeric powder , keep aside.
  3. Peel and cut the potatoes into small cubes, chop the onions, chillies, corainder leaves.
  4. Heat oil and put chana dal, urad dal, mustard seeds, peanuts, curry leaves and fry until they crackle.
  5. Add potatoes , saute for few minutes, then add chopped onions, chillies.
  6. Cook till they are done. Add the poha, corainder leaves and stir.
  7. Keep it on slow flame for 5- 7 minutes.
  8. Let it cool for sometime and add then lemon juice.
  9. Heat the oil in a heavy pan, cook the onion, garlic and red pepper till soft.
  10. Add the tomato and cook another 10 minutes.
  11. Add the tomato paste, salt and pepper to taste.
  12. Cover and let simmer for 15 minutes.
  13. Allow to cool slightly and put the mixture in a blender until it is smooth.
  14. Return to the pan and heat gradually.
  15. Add the fresh basil to the tomato soup, cook 2 minutes and serve.

Upma


Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup Rava / Sooji (Semolina)
  2. 25 gms fried Cashew Nuts (optional)
  3. 1 inch Ginger chopped
  4. 1 chopped Onion
  5. 3 Green Chillies slit sideways
  6. 1 Potato chopped
  7. 1 Capsicum chopped
  8. 1 Carrot chopped
  9. 1/4 cup Green Peas frozen or fresh
  10. 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  11. 1 tsp Urad Daal
  12. 1 tsp Channa Daal
  13. Salt to taste
  14. 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  15. Chili powder to taste (optional)
  16. 2 tblsp Oil
  17. Few curry leaves
  18. Finely chopped corianderleaves
  19. 1 tblsp Ghee
  20. Lemon juice to taste

Procedure:

  1. Sift rava through a muslin / cheese cloth or very fine sieve.
  2. Heat 1tbsp. pure ghee / unsalted butter and fry rava ,on a moderate heat, stiring constantly to light brown color and set aside.
  3. Now heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and allow them to splatter.
  4. Add the daals : channa & urad & curry leaves to it and fry till they turn red.
  5. Add onion, ginger and green chilies. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add all the vegetables, turmeric & chili powder, and salt to taste.
  7. Now add 3 cups of water and cover the pan and allow it simmer on low heat until the vegetables are done.
  8. Add the fried rava to it stirring constantly till it becomes little thick.
  9. Take off from the heat and lemon juice if desired.
  10. Serve hot garnished with cashews and coriander.

Bhindi sabji


Ingredients:

  1. 2 pounds bhindi
  2. 2 onions (chopped)
  3. 1 tblsp ginger garlic paste
  4. 1 tblsp coriander powder
  5. 1 tsp cumin seeds
  6. 1 tsp red chilly powder
  7. 1 tsp garam masala powder
  8. 1 tsp amchur powder
  9. 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  10. salt to taste
  11. oil

Procedure:

  1. Wash and cut bhindi in halves.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or kadai.Add cumin seeds and let it splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and ginger garlic paste.Fry till golden brown.
  4. Now add cut bhindi with rest off the spices and stir well.
  5. Cook it on medium flame and keep stirring it ocassionaly so it does not stick at bottom.
  6. Keep feying till bhindis are cooked.
  7. Serve bhindi masala hot with chapatis.
